Business & Data Analytics
Positions in this sub-family are responsible for providing business and data analytics services to clients. Activities include defining data requirements, data collection, processing, cleaning, analysis, modeling and visualization; developing new insights and understanding of business performance based on data and statistical methods; analyzing external market dynamics and other data sources to assess trends and develop actionable insights; and using data, statistical and quantitative analysis, explanatory and predictive modeling, and fact-based management to drive decision making.
Data Science
Positions in this sub-family are responsible for creating data mining architectures/models/protocols, statistical reporting, and data analysis methodologies to identify trends in large data sets. Activities include researching and applying knowledge of existing and emerging data science principles, theories, and techniques to inform business decisions; conducting economic modeling, statistical analysis, and market surveys; developing tools and theories to facilitate ongoing research activities; and at higher career levels, possibly conducting scientific research projects with the goal of breaking new ground in data analytics.
